The story of Lonnie Builds Things!

A story of how one man seized his piece of the American Dream.


Hello, my name is Lonnie, and I look forward to working with you to make your custom project dreams come true!


I am Indiana born and raised, and my career started like so many others. I worked factory jobs in all areas, including management and upper management. I was working a factory job when Corona hit. Overnight my factory went from 96 employees to 12. I was one of the ones to be let go.


A few months into lockdown, while everyone was watching the world come to a pause, a family friend sent me a picture on Facebook. It was a wood burnt flag, and she wanted to know if I could make her one.


I figured out what materials I would need, and I bought the materials for 5.


My mother and father are telling people that I'm making wood burnt flags now. All five were gone before I completed them. So, I made eight more. Then, I made 15 more.


At this moment, my mother suggested I try the Shipshewana flea market in Shipshewana, Indiana. She's a big fan of the Amish, so I assumed she just wanted to spend time with them for the day.


I took 50 pieces of inventory with me.


I came home with 6.


It was at that moment I felt like I was on to something. I was right. Becoming my own boss and owning my own business has been the best thing to ever happen to me. I can spend time with family and friends making these flags, and I have appreciated every memory we have made doing so.


I donate numerous pieces to foundations and fundraisers each year.


I set up at multiple fairs, festivals, and flea markets throughout the year.


I always look forward to meeting and having conversations with people.
